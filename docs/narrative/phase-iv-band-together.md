## Scene 1 — Crossroads, dusk (Kojo alone)

**Kojo** watches Imray's constables reload. Bonne's boycott already cracked the economy; today cracks fear.

> *"They marched with empty hands… If they want a riot, I'll give them something worse — a plan."*

**Unlocks:** Kojo as protagonist / cell founder.

---

## Scene 2 — Salaga Market, night (Kojo + Araba)

**Araba** tends **cousin Kwame** — leg taken in secondary volleys, not his life.

> **Araba:** *"When you need a door broken, find me."*  
> **Kojo:** *"When I need the ground to shake, I'll know where to stand."*

**Unlocks:** Araba as enforcer ally.

---

## Scene 3 — Kaneshie, dawn (Kojo + Araba + Kofi)

First AWAM fires. **Kofi**, 18, frozen by Crossroads guilt, nearly caught by constables.

Kojo names the **Kofi / Kofi Asare** distinction explicitly so players never confuse Phase I–III clerk with Phase IV rookie.

> **Kofi:** *"I want to do more than run."*  
> **Kojo:** *"You will. But you'll learn before you lead."*

**Unlocks:** Kofi — Fast Learner arc begins.

---

## Scene 4 — Ussher yard, night (Yaw enters)

**Yaw** refuses the front line. His **grandfather** marched unarmed, wounded beside the men who fell.

> **Yaw:** *"I do not march with you… You'll get tools. And my grandfather's rule — hit AWAM stores, not Auntie Esi's fish table."*

He teases **Kwesi** at the Secretariat — setting up Scene 5.

**Unlocks:** Yaw as hub mentor (upgrades, briefings).

---

## Scene 5 — Victoriaborg lane, midnight (Kwesi joins)

**Kwesi** brings patrol manifests and Imray's suppression order. **Odartey Lamptey** was family.

> **Kwesi:** *"I will not carry a gun through it."*  
> **Kojo:** *"When they catch you — we come. All of us."*

**Condition to join:** Document Crossroads truth for the world, not just market gossip.

**Unlocks:** Kwesi — non-combat intel role.

---

## Scene 6 — The oath (full cell)

Command chain sworn before first Phase IV mission:

| Role | Name |
|------|------|
| 1st in Command | **Kojo** — plans, flanks, orders |
| 2nd in Command | **Araba** — breaks lines if Kojo trapped |
| Squad | **Kofi** — learns fast, suppresses |
| Support | **Kwesi** — wiretap, vaults, no combat |
| Mentor | **Yaw** — yard, tools, memory |

Closing narrator line:

> *"Accra burns five days. They burn with purpose."*
